about=QGIS Embeddings AI brings the power of geospatial foundation models to QGIS.
    Foundation models are AI systems pre-trained on massive geospatial datasets that encode
    Earth observations into vector embeddings - compact mathematical representations capturing
    spatial, temporal, and semantic patterns.
    
    Currently supports AlphaEarth (Google DeepMind) with more foundation models coming soon.
    
    CAPABILITIES:
    - Similarity search across global imagery
    - Zero-shot classification and segmentation
    - Multi-modal analysis (optical, radar, elevation, climate)
    - Text-to-map queries via semantic embeddings
    
    REQUIREMENTS:
    - Install the 'Google Earth Engine' plugin from QGIS Plugin Manager
    - Authenticate with your Google account
    - Connect your Google Cloud Project with Earth Engine API enabled
